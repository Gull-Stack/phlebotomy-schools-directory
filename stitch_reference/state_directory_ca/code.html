California Certification Requirements

To practice in California, you must obtain a **Certified Phlebotomy Technician (CPT I or CPT II)** certificate issued by Laboratory Field Services (LFS) within the California Department of Public Health (CDPH). This requires 40 hours of basic didactic, 40 hours of advanced didactic, and 40 hours of clinical practice.
